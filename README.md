## Features — Detailed Guide<br><br>

### 1. Financial Calculators (6 Tools)<br><br>
Do quick math for your financial planning:<br><br>

| Calculator | What It Calculates |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **EMI Calculator** | Monthly payment for a loan (home loan, car loan, etc.) |
| **SIP Calculator** | How much your monthly investments will grow over time |
| **Compound Interest** | Future value of a lump sum investment (supports INR, USD, GBP, and more) |
| **Risk Reward Ratio** | Check if a trade is worth the risk |
| **Budget Calculator** | Income minus expenses = how much you can save each month |
| **Savings Goal** | How many months you need to save for a goal (bike, phone, emergency fund) |

<br>All results are saved automatically so you can see them later on your Dashboard.<br><br>

### 2. Learning Tracker<br><br>
Each guide page (Investments, Trading, Banking, Risks, Calculators) has a **"Mark as Completed"** button. Click it when you finish reading.<br><br>
Your Dashboard shows:<br>
- How many guides you have completed<br>
- A progress bar for each topic<br>
- Overall learning progress in percentage<br><br>

### 3. Badges (Gamification)<br><br>
Earn badges as you learn and use the website:<br><br>

| Badge | How to Earn |
|-------|------------|
| **SIP Beginner** | Complete the Investments guide |
| **Scam Aware** | Complete the Scam Awareness guide |
| **Budget Master** | Use the Budget Calculator or check your Finance Score |
| **Risk Manager** | Complete the Trading guide or score 2+ in the Quiz |
| **Expense Tracker** | Add at least one expense in the Dashboard |

<br>Badges appear in your Dashboard and motivate you to keep learning.<br><br>

### 4. Personal Finance Score<br><br>
Answer a few questions about your income, savings, debt, and spending habits. The system gives you a score out of 100:<br><br>
- **Excellent (75+)** — You are doing great!<br>
- **Good (45-74)** — You are on the right track<br>
- **Poor (below 45)** — Some areas need improvement<br><br>
Your score is saved and shown on your Dashboard.<br><br>

### 5. Finance Quiz<br><br>
Test your knowledge with 3 simple questions:<br>
1. What does IPO mean?<br>
2. What is SIP best known for?<br>
3. What does stop loss help with?<br><br>
Get a score out of 3. Try again anytime to improve your score.<br><br>

### 6. Expense Tracker<br><br>
Log your daily expenses by category:<br>
- Food, Travel, Bills, Shopping, Learning<br><br>
The system:<br>
- Shows your total expenses<br>
- Displays a bar chart so you can see where your money goes<br>
- Saves everything to your Dashboard<br><br>

### 7. Goal Planner<br><br>
Plan your savings for:<br>
- Bike, Phone, Emergency Fund, Education<br><br>
Tell the system your goal amount and how many months you have. It tells you exactly how much to save each month. A savings growth chart shows your progress.<br><br>

### 8. AI Style Advisor (Demo)<br><br>
Select your risk level (Low / Medium / High) and how many years you want to invest. The advisor suggests an investment plan:<br><br>
- **Low Risk** → FD + SIP + Emergency Fund<br>
- **Medium Risk** → Index Fund SIP + Gold + Emergency Fund<br>
- **High Risk** → Stocks + ETF + SIP with risk control<br><br>
> This is a demo for education. Real investing needs more research.<br><br>

### 9. Support Ticket System<br><br>
Need help? Fill out the Support form with:<br>
- Your name, email, phone<br>
- Category (Investing, Trading, Banking, Scams, Calculators)<br>
- Subject and description<br>
- Optional screenshot upload<br><br>
You get a ticket ID like **FV-2026-XXXX**. Track your ticket status on the Dashboard (Pending → In Review → Resolved).<br><br>

### 10. News Dashboard<br><br>
Read 8 demo finance news articles across categories:<br>
- Forex, Stock Market, IPO, Mutual Funds, Crypto, Economy, Gold, Commodities<br><br>
You can:<br>
- **Search** news by typing keywords<br>
- **Filter** by category using the dropdown<br>
- **Save favorites** — click "Save Favorite" to keep articles you like<br><br>
There is also an embedded Investing.com economic calendar for real-world data.<br><br>

### 11. Global Search<br><br>
A search button appears in the toolbar on every page. Click it to search across 10 topics:<br>
- IPO, SIP, Stocks, Stop Loss, Credit Card Traps, UPI Safety, Fake Trading Gurus, EMI Calculator, SIP Calculator, Finance News<br><br>
Click any result to go directly to the relevant page.<br><br>

## Theme System<br><br>

Themes are applied using CSS variables. When you select a theme, it adds a class to the `<body>` tag and saves your preference in localStorage.<br><br>

| Theme | CSS Class | What changes |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| Light Mode | `theme-cream` | Default look — cream bg, navy text, gold accents |
| Blue Theme | `theme-blue` | Blue-tinted light background |
| White Clean | `theme-clean` | Pure white background |
| Dark Mode | `theme-dark` | Dark bg, light text, gold highlights |

<br>---<br><br>

## How to Customize<br><br>

| What to Change | Where to Edit |
|---------------|--------------|
| Colors & themes | `CSS/style.css` — edit the `:root` and `.theme-*` variables |
| News articles | `JAVA SCRIPT/script.js` — update the `financeNews` array |
| Search topics | `JAVA SCRIPT/script.js` — update the `searchTopics` array |
| Social media links | Footer in each HTML file (Telegram, Instagram, YouTube) |
| Logo | Replace `IMAGE/Financeverse.png` with your own image |
| Website name | Change "FinanceVerse" in all HTML files and the logo |
