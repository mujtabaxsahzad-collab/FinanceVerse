Banking Guide

Use banking tools without falling into traps

Cards, EMI, UPI, net banking, BNPL, and personal loans can help when used carefully.

Debit Card Use

A debit card spends money directly from your bank account. Keep daily limits sensible and never share PIN or OTP.

Credit Card Smart Usage

Use credit cards for planned spending and pay the full bill before the due date to avoid interest.

Credit Card Traps

Minimum due payments, cash withdrawals, late fees, and reward chasing can make credit cards expensive.

EMI

EMI divides a purchase or loan into monthly payments. Check interest, processing fees, and total cost before agreeing.

UPI Safety

Verify receiver names, avoid unknown collect requests, and remember that receiving money does not require entering your PIN.

Net Banking Safety

Use official bank websites, strong passwords, and two-factor authentication. Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive banking.

Buy Now Pay Later Traps

BNPL can make small purchases feel harmless. Missed payments may create fees and hurt your credit behavior.

Personal Loan Traps

Personal loans can carry high interest. Avoid borrowing for lifestyle spending or to repay repeated impulsive debt.
