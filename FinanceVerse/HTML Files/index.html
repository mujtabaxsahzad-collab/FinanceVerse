Daily Money Choices

Good habits vs bad habits

Good Habits

  • Budget before spending.
  • Save before upgrading lifestyle.
  • Invest only after learning the risk.
  • Read loan and card terms carefully.

Bad Habits

  • Borrowing for wants.
  • Following random trading tips.
  • Ignoring emergency savings.
  • Chasing quick rich schemes.
